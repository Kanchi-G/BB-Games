Internship Program for Software Developers

We are starting a 3 months Internship Program for Software Developers. In this program the intern will learn to develop mobile application while working on live projects.

Why the program is beneficial for you?

In our engineering colleges, the education given is not enough for the corporate world. Students learn more theory than practical stuff and are not prepared for the real world. What we are giving you is an opportunity of 3 months to work on live projects. We give you specific projects to do so that you learn new technologies.

After 3 months internship, you will be considered for a full time job at IDZ itself. If you don't get selected here, you still get an internship certificate and valuable experience

Note that there will be no trainers and this is not a coaching class. What will happen is that you will be given specific tasks in a variety of different platforms and you will be expected to work around the tutorials on your own. Guidance will be given whenever asked for.
